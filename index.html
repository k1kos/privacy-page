Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Fesari Ad Data ("we", "our", or "us") operates an internal tool to fetch public advertising data from Meta's Ad Library API for research and transparency purposes. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use data through our application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data from users. All data accessed through our tool comes from Meta's publicly available Ad Library API.

Use of Meta Platform Data

Our application only accesses public data made available by Meta through the Ad Library API. We comply with Meta Platform Terms and use this data solely for research, monitoring, and transparency analysis related to public advertisements.

Cookies and Tracking

Our application does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data collected via the Meta API with third parties.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect our API credentials and access tokens, ensuring that public data is accessed securely.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, you can contact us at: contact@fesari.com

Terms of Service

This tool is intended for internal use for transparency and research purposes only. We do not collect or sell user data, and the application is not intended for public consumer use or commercial advertising.

User Data Deletion

We do not store personal user data. If you believe we do and wish to request deletion, please contact us at contact@fesari.com with your request.
